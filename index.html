About

I do research on the molecular biology of plant circadian rhythms. I want to understand how biological clocks work so I can build custom plant clocks fine-tuned for climate change and extra-terrestrial environments.

Previously, I studied mosquito ecology and malaria transmission.

I made this website to compile some of the things related to my interests.
